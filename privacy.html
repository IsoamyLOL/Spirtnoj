Privacy Policy for Spirtnoj

Last Updated: 11-29-2023

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Spirtnoj for iOS.

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Application

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Spirtnoj for iOS. Individual settings relating to the Spirtnoj app are not personal and are stored only on your device.

Calculations and Data

Spirtnoj performs calculations based on user input regarding spirit volume, spirit strength, total spirit volume, and desired total spirit strength. These calculations are done using specialized russian state standard (GOST) tables, which involve interpolation to provide specific results. All data entered by the user for these calculations is processed locally on the user's device and is not collected, stored, or shared by us.

No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising frameworks in Spirtnoj.
